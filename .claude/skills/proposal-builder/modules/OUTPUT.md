# Output Module

Generate proposal documents from discovery and solution design.

## Client-Facing Proposal Structure

```
# Proposal: {Client Name} - Outbound Campaign

## Your Situation
{2-3 sentences summarizing their current state and challenges}

## What We'll Do
{Scope of work in plain language}

## Expected Results
{Projected outcomes with specific numbers}

## Timeline
{Week-by-week breakdown for first month, then monthly}

## Investment
{Pricing table}

## Next Steps
{Clear call to action}
```

## Internal Proposal Structure

Same as client-facing, plus:
- Assumptions and confidence levels
- Competitive analysis (who else they might be talking to)
- Risk factors
- Discovery questions for follow-up call
- ROI calculation

## Rules

- Client-facing version: under 2 pages
- No jargon: "prospect list" not "TAM build"
- Always include specific numbers in expected results
- Show ROI calculation: investment vs. projected pipeline value
