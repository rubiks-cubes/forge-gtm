# Outreach Output Style

Rules for all outbound email copy produced by this workspace.

## Format

### Email Structure
- 4 emails per sequence
- 2 threads, 2 emails each
- Thread 1: Primary angle (Email 1.1 + 1.2)
- Thread 2: Secondary angle (Email 2.1 + 2.2)

### Subject Lines
- 3-4 words maximum
- All lowercase (except proper nouns)
- No punctuation
- Email 1.1 and 2.1: unique subjects
- Email 1.2 and 2.2: EMPTY (auto-threads with previous email)

### Body Rules
- Maximum 75 words per email
- One idea per email
- First line about THEM, not about you
- No self-introductions
- Short sentences, use line breaks
- Include SPINTAX for variation

### CTA Rules
- Question format only (never "book a call")
- Soft and curiosity-based
- One CTA per email
- Email 2.2: include referral angle ("Would {other_contact} be better to chat with?")

## Tone

- Peer-to-peer (not salesperson-to-prospect)
- Confident but not pushy
- Specific and data-driven
- Human, not corporate

## Variables

Standard: {{first_name}}, {{company_name}}, {{title}}
Custom: {{custom_variable}} for per-lead personalization

## Quality Gate

Before finalizing any sequence, verify:
1. No patterns from BANNED.md
2. All emails under 75 words
3. SPINTAX present for variation
4. Two distinct angles across threads
5. Specific numbers (not vague claims)
