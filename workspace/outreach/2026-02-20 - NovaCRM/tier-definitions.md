# Tier Definitions: NovaCRM

**Product:** Modern CRM for B2B SaaS sales teams
**Date:** 2026-02-20
**Based on:** product-profile.md

---

## Tier 1: In-Market NOW

**Expected response rate:** 15-25%

### Criteria
- Company has a job posting for "Salesforce Administrator" or "CRM Administrator" (EDP)
- OR: New VP Sales / CRO hired in the last 6 months AND company uses Salesforce
- Company size: 50-500 employees
- Industry: B2B SaaS / Technology

### Where to Find
- **LinkedIn Jobs:** Search "Salesforce Administrator" at companies with 50-500 employees in SaaS
- **LinkedIn People:** Filter VP Sales / CRO with "started new position" in last 6 months
- **Indeed/Glassdoor:** "CRM admin" postings at SaaS companies

### Volume Estimate
~200-400 companies at any given time in the US

### Messaging Approach
Direct and specific. Reference the exact trigger: "Saw you're hiring a Salesforce admin. That role exists because the tool is too complex. What if you didn't need that role?"

---

## Tier 2: Approaching

**Expected response rate:** 5-10%

### Criteria
- Company posted 3+ sales roles in the last 90 days (growing sales team)
- OR: Raised Series A-C in the last 12 months
- Company size: 50-500 employees
- Currently using Salesforce or HubSpot (tech stack signal)

### Where to Find
- **LinkedIn Jobs:** Companies with multiple sales openings
- **Crunchbase:** Recent funding rounds filtered by industry and size
- **BuiltWith/HG Insights:** Salesforce or HubSpot usage data

### Volume Estimate
~1,000-2,000 companies in the US

### Messaging Approach
Educational. "Companies scaling from 5 to 15 reps hit a CRM wall. The tool that worked at 5 reps becomes a bottleneck at 15. Here's what DataSync did about it."

---

## Tier 3: ICP Fit

**Expected response rate:** 1-3%

### Criteria
- B2B SaaS company, 50-500 employees
- VP Sales, CRO, or Sales Director as decision maker
- US or Canada based
- No specific buying trigger detected

### Where to Find
- **Apollo/LinkedIn Sales Nav:** Standard firmographic filters
- **Industry databases:** SaaS company directories

### Volume Estimate
~5,000-10,000 companies in the US

### Messaging Approach
Proof-point led. "DataSync switched from Salesforce to NovaCRM. 47% less data entry, $36K saved, 2-week setup. Worth a look if your CRM renewal is coming up?"
