# PeakHire - Target ICP

## Primary Segment: Mid-Market HR Leaders

| Attribute | Criteria |
|-----------|----------|
| **Title** | VP People, Head of Talent, HR Director, CHRO |
| **Industry** | Technology, Professional Services, Financial Services |
| **Company size** | 200-2,000 employees |
| **Location** | United States |
| **Signal** | Actively hiring (10+ open roles on careers page) |

### Pain Points

1. Recruiter burnout - screening hundreds of unqualified candidates
2. Time to hire is too long (60+ days for technical roles)
3. Existing ATS is a database, not a recruiting assistant
4. Diversity hiring goals but no tools to reduce bias in screening
5. Spending $100K+/year on recruiting agencies for hard-to-fill roles

### Buying Triggers

- VP People or CHRO just hired (reviewing recruiting stack)
- Company in rapid hiring mode (10+ open roles)
- Recent layoffs at competitors (talent suddenly available)
- Budget season / annual planning cycle

## Exclusion Criteria

- Companies under 200 employees (product not built for small teams)
- Staffing agencies (different use case)
- Government or education (long procurement cycles)
