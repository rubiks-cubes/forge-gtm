# PeakHire - Offer Positioning

## Product

PeakHire is an AI-powered recruiting platform that screens and ranks candidates automatically. "Like having 5 extra recruiters who never sleep."

## Key Value Proposition

Cut your time-to-hire in half. PeakHire's AI screens every applicant in real-time, ranks them by fit, and surfaces the top 10% so your recruiters only talk to qualified candidates.

## Proof Points

- Beta customer (TechRow, 400 employees): reduced time-to-hire from 58 days to 29 days
- 73% reduction in recruiter screening time
- 2.4x improvement in candidate quality scores

## Campaign Angle

Lead with the recruiter burnout pain point. HR leaders know the problem viscerally. Position PeakHire as "relief for your recruiting team, not replacement."
