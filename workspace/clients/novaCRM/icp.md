# NovaCRM - Target ICP

## Primary Segment: SMB Sales Leaders

| Attribute | Criteria |
|-----------|----------|
| **Title** | VP Sales, Head of Sales, Sales Director, CRO |
| **Industry** | B2B SaaS, Software, Technology |
| **Company size** | 50-500 employees |
| **Revenue** | $5M-$50M ARR |
| **Location** | United States, Canada |
| **Tech stack signal** | Using Salesforce or HubSpot CRM |

### Pain Points

1. Current CRM is bloated and expensive (Salesforce fatigue)
2. Sales reps spend too much time on data entry
3. Pipeline visibility is poor - forecasting is guesswork
4. Onboarding new reps takes 3+ months because the CRM is complex
5. Paying for features they don't use

### Buying Triggers

- New VP Sales or CRO hired (reviewing tech stack)
- Recent fundraise (budget to upgrade tools)
- Growing sales team (need scalable CRM)
- Salesforce contract renewal coming up

## Secondary Segment: Growth-Stage Founders

| Attribute | Criteria |
|-----------|----------|
| **Title** | CEO, Founder, Co-Founder |
| **Industry** | B2B SaaS |
| **Company size** | 20-100 employees |
| **Revenue** | $1M-$10M ARR |
| **Signal** | First sales hires or transitioning from founder-led sales |

### Why They Buy

- Need a CRM that grows with them without enterprise complexity
- Want something their small team can actually adopt
- Price-sensitive compared to Salesforce

## Exclusion Criteria

- Companies already using NovaCRM (existing customers)
- Companies with fewer than 20 employees (too early)
- Non-SaaS companies (product not built for them)
- Companies in healthcare or government (compliance requirements NovaCRM doesn't meet yet)
