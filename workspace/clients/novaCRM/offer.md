# NovaCRM - Offer Positioning

## Product

NovaCRM is a modern CRM built for B2B SaaS sales teams. Positioned as "the CRM that actually helps you sell."

## Key Differentiators

| Feature | NovaCRM | Salesforce | HubSpot |
|---------|---------|------------|---------|
| Setup time | 2 weeks | 3-6 months | 1-2 months |
| Price (50 users) | $4,000/mo | $7,500/mo | $5,000/mo |
| AI forecasting | Built-in | Add-on ($$$) | Limited |
| Data entry | Auto-capture | Manual heavy | Semi-auto |

## Value Proposition

Switch from Salesforce to NovaCRM. Cut your CRM costs by 40%, get your reps selling instead of typing, and actually trust your pipeline forecast.
