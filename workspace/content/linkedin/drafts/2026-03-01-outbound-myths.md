**Topic:** Common outbound myths
**Status:** Draft

---

Most companies think outbound doesn't work anymore.

They're wrong. Bad outbound doesn't work anymore.

Here's what we see across 15+ B2B SaaS clients:

The ones who fail:
- Blast 50,000 generic emails
- Measure success by open rates
- Copy-paste the same sequence for every segment

The ones booking 30+ meetings/month:
- Build lists of 1,000-2,000 highly targeted prospects
- Write different messaging for different pain points
- Measure meetings and pipeline, not vanity metrics

The difference isn't the channel. It's the approach.

Outbound works. Lazy outbound doesn't.
