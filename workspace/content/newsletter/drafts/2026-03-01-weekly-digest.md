# Weekly Digest - March 1, 2026

**Subject line:** The Salesforce renewal play (and why it works)

---

One of our best-performing campaigns right now targets companies whose Salesforce contract is up for renewal.

Why it works:

1. **Timing is everything.** These companies are already evaluating alternatives. You're not creating demand - you're capturing it.

2. **The pain is fresh.** Annual renewal is when the sticker shock hits. "$150K for a CRM our reps hate using."

3. **The case study lands.** When we say "DataSync saved $36K and cut data entry by 47%," they're doing the math in their head.

If you sell B2B software that competes with an incumbent, try this: build a list of companies using the competitor, filter by contract renewal timing, and lead with a case study of someone who switched.

It's the simplest campaign structure we run. And it's one of the highest-performing.
