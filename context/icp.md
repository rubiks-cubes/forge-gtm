# Forge GTM - Ideal Client Profile

## Primary ICP: B2B SaaS Companies (Series A-B)

| Attribute | Criteria |
|-----------|----------|
| Stage | Series A or B (raised $5M-$30M) |
| Team size | 30-200 employees |
| Revenue | $2M-$20M ARR |
| Sales team | 3-15 reps |
| Current outbound | Doing some, but inconsistent or underperforming |
| Decision maker | VP Sales, Head of Growth, CRO, or Founder |

### Why This Segment

- Have budget for outbound services ($3K-$12K/month)
- Big enough to need help, small enough to move fast
- Usually have product-market fit but struggle with repeatable pipeline
- Founder or sales leader still involved in GTM decisions

### Pain Signals

1. Sales team spending 40%+ of time on prospecting instead of closing
2. Inconsistent pipeline month-to-month
3. Tried outbound in-house, results were mediocre
4. Hiring SDRs but can't keep them productive
5. Relying too heavily on inbound or referrals

## Secondary ICP: Marketing/Growth Agencies

| Attribute | Criteria |
|-----------|----------|
| Team size | 5-30 people |
| Revenue | $500K-$5M |
| Services | SEO, paid media, content - want to add outbound |
| Decision maker | Founder or Head of Delivery |

### Why This Segment

- Want to expand service offering without hiring outbound specialists
- Can white-label our campaigns for their clients
- Referral-heavy, one good partnership generates multiple accounts

## Who Is NOT Our Client

- Pre-revenue startups (no budget, unclear ICP)
- Enterprise companies (too slow, need RFPs and procurement)
- Companies selling to consumers (B2C)
- Anyone wanting "just a list" with no campaign management
