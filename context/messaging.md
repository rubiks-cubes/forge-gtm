# Forge GTM - Messaging

## One-Line Pitch

We build outbound pipeline for B2B SaaS companies so their sales teams can focus on closing instead of prospecting.

## Core Value Propositions

### 1. Pipeline Without the Headcount

Most companies hire 2-3 SDRs to run outbound. That's $150K-$300K/year in salary, training, and management overhead. We deliver the same (or better) pipeline at a fraction of the cost.

### 2. Speed to Pipeline

In-house outbound takes 3-6 months to ramp. We launch campaigns in 2 weeks and book meetings in month one.

### 3. Quality Over Volume

We don't blast 50,000 generic emails. We build targeted lists of 1,000-2,000 qualified prospects per campaign and write messaging that actually resonates with their situation.

## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We tried outbound, it didn't work" | "What was your reply rate? Most in-house efforts get 0.5-1%. We consistently hit 3-5% because we invest in list quality and personalization." |
| "We prefer inbound" | "Inbound is great when it's flowing. Outbound gives you control over pipeline volume regardless of content performance or algorithm changes." |
| "Too expensive" | "Compare it to an SDR: $75K salary + tools + management time + 3-month ramp. We deliver results from day one at less than half the cost." |
| "We need to do this in-house" | "We can help you build the playbook. Start with us, learn what works, then bring it in-house with a proven system." |
