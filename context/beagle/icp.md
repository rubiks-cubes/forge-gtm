# Beagle Security — Ideal Customer Profile

## Primary ICP: Security-Aware Engineering Leaders

These are the people who feel the problem most directly.

| Attribute | Criteria |
|-----------|----------|
| **Titles** | CTO, VP Engineering, Head of Engineering, DevSecOps Lead, Platform Lead |
| **Secondary titles** | CISO, Head of Security, AppSec Engineer (influencer, not buyer) |
| **Industries** | B2B SaaS, Fintech, Healthtech, E-commerce, EdTech |
| **Company size** | 20–500 employees |
| **Stage** | Seed+ (post-product-market fit, pre-enterprise maturity) |
| **Geography** | US (primary), UK, India, Australia |
| **Tech signals** | CI/CD pipeline in use (GitHub Actions, CircleCI, Jenkins), API-first architecture |

### Pain Profile

- Running annual or ad-hoc pen tests with agencies — slow, expensive, not continuous
- Vulnerability scanners producing too many false positives, developers ignoring reports
- Security not integrated into development workflow — bolted on before releases
- Compliance (SOC 2, HIPAA, PCI DSS) driving a pen testing need, no good automated option
- No dedicated security team — engineering team owns security alongside everything else

### Why They Buy Beagle

- Continuous automated testing without hiring a security team
- Reports developers and executives can both read and act on
- Compliance coverage built in — SOC 2, HIPAA, PCI DSS without extra steps
- Plugs into existing CI/CD — doesn't require changing how the team works

## Buyer Tiers

### Tier 1 — In-Market NOW (Expected reply rate: 15–25%)

Observable signals that they need this right now:

| Signal | Where to Find |
|--------|--------------|
| Hiring "Security Engineer", "AppSec Engineer", or "Penetration Tester" | LinkedIn Jobs, Greenhouse, Lever |
| SOC 2 or ISO 27001 certification page live on their site | Company website /security or /trust |
| Series A or B funding announced (last 90 days) | Crunchbase, TechCrunch |
| New CISO, VP Engineering, or CTO hired (last 60 days) | LinkedIn |
| Public mention of audit, compliance, or security initiative | Blog, press release, LinkedIn post |

**EDP (Existential Data Point):** Job posting for a Security Engineer. They've recognized the need, they're about to spend $140–180K on a hire, and Beagle automates most of what that hire would do.

### Tier 2 — Approaching (Expected reply rate: 5–10%)

Leading indicators — they'll need this within 3–6 months:

| Signal | Where to Find |
|--------|--------------|
| Engineering headcount grew 30%+ in 6 months | LinkedIn headcount trends |
| Adopted CI/CD tooling recently (GitHub Actions, Terraform, Docker) | BuiltWith, Stackshare |
| Fintech / Healthtech and growing — regulatory pressure building | Industry context |
| Competitor in their space had a publicized breach | NewsAPI, Google Alerts |

### Tier 3 — ICP Fit (Expected reply rate: 1–3%)

Right profile, no active trigger. Plant the seed.

- B2B SaaS / Fintech / Healthtech / E-commerce
- 20–500 employees
- CTO, VP Eng, Head of Security title
- No specific signal detected

## Exclusion Criteria

- Companies already using Beagle Security (check before prospecting)
- Hardware, manufacturing, non-software companies
- Government and public sector (compliance complexity we don't serve yet)
- Companies under 15 employees (too early, no budget)
- Agencies and consulting firms (they're potential partners, not customers)
